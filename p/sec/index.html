Security

You may need to install this on your browser; I prefer Firefox and Brave Browser.

Extensions

  • uBlock Origin - an effective blocker. CPU and memory requirements are minimised.
  • Decentraleyes - Protects you from tracking with "free", centralised content delivery.
  • Privacy Badger - Privacy Badger automatically learns to detect and block invisible trackers.
  • Malwarebytes Browser Guard - Malwarebytes Browser Guard provides a safer and faster web browsing experience. It disables trackers and harmful websites while removing intrusive adverts and other undesirable content, such as credit card skimmers.
  • Canvas Fingerprinting Defender - Defending against canvas fingerprinting by reporting a false value.
  • FastForward - FastForward is the successor to Universal circumvent, a browser plugin that can circumvent bothersome link shorteners, saving you time trying to reach your destination link.

    • VPNs (You can choose any one of these)

  • ProtonVPN - The most secure VPN for browsing secretly and accessing prohibited material. Developed by CERN scientists and protected by Swiss privacy regulations.
  • NordVPN - Leading VPN service that encrypts your internet traffic and conceals your IP address and physical location. Upgrade your privacy and security today.
  • ExpressVPN - VPN offers 256-bit encryption, 94 countries, and no logging. It is also ranked as one of the fastest VPNs available.
  • WindScribe - Windscribe is a desktop application and browser extension that work together to prevent advertisements and trackers while restoring access to prohibited content.
